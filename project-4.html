Cricket Scorekeeper App

Skills: Swift (including SwiftUI and CoreData)

Description: This was my first ever iOS app. It allowed me to keep track of the score of a cricket match, and is designed for simplicity and intuitiveness. It enables real-time scoring with minimal taps, and it saves the scorecards of every match to the device, so any match can be revisited at any time.

Inspiration: My friends and I regularly play cricket, and during our matches, we found it cumbersome to keep track of the score, because we all wanted to focus on actually playing the game.
